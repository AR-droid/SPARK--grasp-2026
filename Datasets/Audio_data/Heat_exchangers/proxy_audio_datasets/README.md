Direct acoustic datasets for heat exchanger faults
(cavitation, fouling, flow instability) are limited.

Therefore, proxy industrial and environmental audio
datasets curated from the AudEERING dataset collection
are used.

Datasets included:
- ESC-50: Environmental and mechanical sound events
- UrbanSound8K: Urban and machinery-related noise
- MUSAN: Noise corpus for background modeling and augmentation

Purpose in SPARK:
- Learn normal industrial background acoustics
- Build anomaly detection baselines
- Support early cavitation and flow-noise detection
